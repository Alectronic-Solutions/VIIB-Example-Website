About Us

Welcome to the world of VIIB, where music meets soul and rhythm tells a story.

Born and raised in the vibrant heart of Cali, VIIB is a band that blends the timeless essence of reggae with the laid-back, sun-soaked vibes of Central California. Our music is a celebration of life, love, and the power of community, inspired by the rich cultural tapestry of our roots.

From the beaches of Santa Cruz to the rolling hills of Sonora, we’ve performed in iconic venues and intimate local spots across Central California, including Stockton, Lodi, Modesto, Sonora, Merced, Turlock, Patterson, and Visalia. Every performance is an opportunity to connect with our audience and spread the message of One Love.

Whether it’s a private gig, a local show, or a community event, we bring the rhythm, energy, and passion that make every moment unforgettable. Our sound is a fusion of classic reggae and the unique spirit of California’s reggae scene, crafted to inspire and uplift.

Join us on this journey as we continue to grow, create, and share the vibes of VIIB. Let’s make music together and keep the spirit of reggae alive!

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book the band?

To book VIIB for your event, please use the contact form in the footer or email us directly at booking@viib.com. Include details like the event date, location, and type of event for a quicker response.

Where can I listen to your music?

Our music is available on all major streaming platforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and Bandcamp. Check out the "Music" section on our website for direct links.

Do you sell merchandise?

Yes! We have a variety of merchandise, including T-shirts, hoodies, and posters, available in the "Shop" section of our website. All items are shipped worldwide.

What genre of music?

VIIB specializes in reggae music, blending classic reggae vibes with the laid-back energy of California. Our sound is a fusion of roots reggae, acoustic melodies, and positive lyrics.

Do you perform at private events?

Yes, we perform at private events such as weddings, corporate parties, and community gatherings. Contact us with your event details, and we’ll provide a customized quote.

How can I stay updated?

Subscribe to our newsletter in the footer or follow us on social media (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter) for the latest updates on new music, tour dates, and more.
